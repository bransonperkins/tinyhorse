We're people who like people.

Tiny Horse is always on the lookout for innovative and creative minds who have a passion for building and entertaining audiences.

Our Culture

We work hard and we play hard. Whether we’re testing out new craft beers made by our local clients, or winning a Cannes Lions for creative campaigns, we’ve built a culture where we can trust each other to deliver. We don’t hesitate in asking each other for help, so if you aren’t afraid to raise your hand and step out of your comfort zone, let’s talk. When we aren’t at the office, it’s not unlikely that we’re hitting up happy hours or singing terrible karaoke on a Saturday night because we genuinely like each other. We seek candidates who want to become part of our family.

We’re self-starters. If micromanagement is your thing, then Tiny Horse probably isn’t for you. Our team feels confident in taking the initiative to get the job done.

But we also value collaboration. We trust the brainpower of every one of our employees, no matter their role, as we believe the sum is greater than the parts. Check your ego at the door, she can’t sit with us. We respect and support each other so that brainstorms can lead to million-dollar idea.

And lastly, we never forget where we came from. We pride ourselves on our unique roots across entertainment, production, and marketing. We’re an eccentric and diverse bunch. Don’t be surprised if you catch a partner dressed in drag. That’s show biz, baby!
